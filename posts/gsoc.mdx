# Relevant links and info for GSOC
- [Orgs from 2021](https://summerofcode.withgoogle.com/archive/2021/organizations)
- [Ideas list from KDE](https://community.kde.org/GSoC/2021/Ideas)
- Choose 1-2 orgs that interest you and are selected for gsoc regularly and go throught their ideas and codebase and start preparing a proposal early
- Max 3 proposals can be submitted (for 2022) and from those 3, 1 will be selected
